# Hodos

**Algorithms are not implemented here — they emerge.**

This site documents the ideas, design principles, and examples behind Hodos.

Hodos is a graph traversal framework where classical algorithms
(BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, A*, …) are not hardcoded, but **emerge from composition**.
